 ~2018~
Ah yes, 2018. I think that as of know, this is my best year. I'm rather proud of the improvements I've made since previous years. (i'm still garbage though compared to some others though) This was the year I actually bothered to start learning music theory, which helped me a lot. There's also a super secret collab album I'm working on with a few others, which might come out late this year, but I wouldn't really know, since I'm not really the one who decides that kind of stuff. This year was just the year of new plugins, although I occasionally revisited retro-sounding soundfonts. Additionally, I finally started learning how to mix! Wow!

 ~2017~
Ugh, 2017. For most of this year, I used primarily soundfonts and occasional stock FL Studio plugins. Speaking of FL, this was the year I got it. Before that, I was using LMMS. In the later quarter of the year, I started attempting to learn how to mix, but it mostly turned out sounding like JynX's bad mixing unsatisfactory. I say I used soundfonts for most of the year, as by the end of the summer, I obtained the EDIROL Hyper Canvas VST, which I used for the Reverie album. Overall, it ranged from terrible to half-decent. Also, there's a few songs that I made in this year that were lost to the menance of hard drive failures. Press F to pay respects.
